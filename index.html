Brenda Brito is an aspiring news reporter/correspondent currently studying telecommunications at the University of Florida, one of the top ten universities for journalism in the nation. During her time at UF, she has worked as a writer, reporter and anchor for Noticias WUFT, a Spanish news radio program targeting the Hispanic community and aired in 17 counties of North Central Florida. She has also worked for WUFT-TV production and Ritmo Latino radio.

Brenda Brito was one of the four News interns for Univision Inc. in Miami and the only one trained for Digital News. During her time at Univision she collaborated to work on the humanitarian crisis at the border and the soccer World Cup 2014. She had the opportunity to intern for NBCUniversal and Mega TV in Miami during her freshman and sophomore year.

This past summer 2014 she traveled to Germany to work on a photojournalism project about the chalk art in Berlin. She is currently an intern at Holbrook Travel in charge of social media content generation and video editing/production for the marketing department.
